VISION
Our vision at Sri Arungaraiamman Nallathaai Vidhyalayam school is to empower students to acquire,demonstrate ,articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as lifelong learners to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school respect,tolerance and inclusion and excellence. Also strives to provide an international educational experience, cultivating character,embracing leadership and inspiring innovation,empowering students to reach their full potential and confidently engage the world positively.
Provide a rigorous and innovative curriculum that best prepares students to succeed in life
Ensure long term financial stability for the school
Provide a school environment that enables students to seek insight or further learning beyond walls
Foster a safe environment and a culture of continuous improvement
Cultivate relationships with colleagues
